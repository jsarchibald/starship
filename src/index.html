How to Play

  1. You are the commander of a spaceship. Your job is to avoid the space debris flying at you. Use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard to move your ship.
  2. For each piece of debris you avoid, you receive a random number of points between 50 and 150. Your score is in the top left corner.
  3. If you are hit by space debris, you will have 20 seconds to conjugate an irregular verb in the passé simple.
  4. You have three lives. You lose one life for each question you answer incorrectly.
  5. You cannot pause the game.
